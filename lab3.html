About this Lab

This page demonstrates a properly structured, semantic HTML5 layout with an accessible form and data table. It includes a keyboard-accessible skip link, a CSS-only dropdown to jump between sections, legible typography via Google Fonts, and Google Material Icons that are hidden from screen readers when decorative.

info

Before submitting, validate with the HTML Validator, WAVE, Silktide, HTML5 Outliner, and Lighthouse. Use VoiceOver (macOS) or NVDA (Windows), turn off/dim your screen, and navigate this page by keyboard to experience it like a screen-reader user.
